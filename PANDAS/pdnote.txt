1️⃣ What groupby means (core idea)

Think:

split → apply → combine

Example:

Group customers by Country
Then calculate something for each group


11️⃣ One very important rule

groupby must be followed by an operation:

Examples:

.mean()
.sum()
.count()
.size()
.max()
.min()
.agg()
Otherwise nothing happens.


Mental model

.agg() simply does:

for each group:
    apply operation to column

So pandas sees:

"mean" → call .mean()
"count" → call .count()


Simple rule
agg({
 column : operation
})
